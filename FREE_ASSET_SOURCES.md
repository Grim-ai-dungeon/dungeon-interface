# Dungeon Interface - Free Asset Sources

*As requested, here is a curated list of high-quality, free assets and AI tools to replace the amateur interface with a professional, detailed sci-fi/cyberpunk isometric style.*

## 1. Isometric Room Tilesets & Objects
*Replaces: Basic grid and room layouts*

*   **Kenney - Sci-Fi Base / Hexagon Kits**
    *   **URL:** [https://kenney.nl/assets/hexagon-kit](https://kenney.nl/assets/hexagon-kit)
    *   **License:** CC0 (Public Domain, Free for commercial use)
    *   **Details:** Great foundation for hexagonal isometric rooms. Can be recolored and combined with other pixel art tools to create detailed floorings.
*   **OpenGameArt - Isometric Sci-Fi Indoor Tiles**
    *   **URL:** [https://opengameart.org/content/isometric-sci-fi-indoor-tiles](https://opengameart.org/content/isometric-sci-fi-indoor-tiles)
    *   **License:** CC0 / CC-BY
    *   **Details:** Floor tiles, walls, and basic computer terminals in an isometric perspective.
*   **Itch.io - Tech / Cyberpunk Interiors (Various Free Packs)**
    *   **URL:** [https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-cyberpunk/tag-isometric](https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-cyberpunk/tag-isometric) (Search tag)
    *   **Details:** Look for "Cyberpunk / Sci-Fi Interior" free packs by creators like *Anokolisa* or *Penzilla* who often offer free versions of their premium tilesets containing glowing monitors, cables, and lab tubes.

## 2. Pre-Made Isometric Room Backgrounds
*Replaces: Plain static backgrounds for specific dungeon rooms (Treasury, Library, Workshop, Command Chamber)*

*   *Note: Highly detailed, pre-made entire room backgrounds for specific sci-fi themes are extremely rare in free asset stores. The best approach for unique, cohesive rooms (Command Chamber, Lab) is using AI Generators (see section 6) to create fixed backgrounds, then layering UI and character sprites on top.*

## 5. AI Tools for Generating Isometric Room Art
*Since hyper-specific, highly detailed sci-fi hexagonal rooms (like the reference) are hard to find pre-made and free, AI tools are the most powerful option to get exact results.*

*   **Midjourney v6**
    *   **Cost:** Paid (but best quality for this specific task)
    *   **Prompt style:** `isometric pixel art, sci-fi cyberpunk command center, hexagonal room layout, detailed interior, glowing neon monitors, holographic displays, trailing cables, lab equipment, dark atmospheric background, video game asset, 8k resolution --v 6.0`
*   **Leonardo.ai**
    *   **Cost:** **Free tier available** (generous daily credits)
    *   **Details:** Extremely good at video game assets. You can use their "Pixel Art" models or isometric 3D models to generate exact room backgrounds for the Library, Treasury, and Workshop.
*   **Scenario.com**
    *   **Cost:** Free tier available
    *   **Details:** Built specifically for game developers. You can generate custom isometric tilesets and props that perfectly match the "underground bunker" style.
*   **Bing Image Creator (DALL-E 3)**
    *   **Cost:** **Free**
    *   **Details:** Great at understanding complex prompts. Try prompting: *"A 2D isometric video game asset of a sci-fi cyberpunk laboratory room, hexagonal floor grid, glowing blue and purple neon lighting, multiple computer monitors, cables, workbenches, dark background, detailed pixel art style."*

---
**Implementation Strategy for the Overlord:**
1. Download **Kenney's Space UI Pack** immediately to reskin the menus and sidebars.
2. Use **Leonardo.ai (Free)** or **Bing Image Creator (Free)** to generate the 5 main room backgrounds (Treasury, Command Chamber, etc.) using the isometric prompts provided above.
3. Layer the UI over the AI-generated isometric backgrounds for an instant, massive leap in visual quality.
